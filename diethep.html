Diet for Hepatits C


  • Whole grains are very beneficial to consume as part of a healthy hepatits diet. These can be in the form of bran, whole wheat bread or cereal, brown rice, whole grain pasta or porridge. Include other whole grains such as whole oats, wild rice,ryce oatmeal & corn.
  • Olive oil, canola oil and flaxseed oil
  • Healthy proteins in the form of low-fat milk and dairy products along with lean meats, beans,eggs & soy products.
  • Fruits and vegetables should be a significant part of the diet as they are full of nutrients and easy to digest.


What to avoid?


  • Processed food items as they are harder onliver and relatively devoid of nutrients. It includes processed breads, cheese and almost all food items.
  • Hydrogenated oils must be replaced with healthy oils.
  • Sugar intake should be limited for a healthy liver. This includes all artificial sweeteners as well as fruit juices.
  • Salt intake should also be limited. Also refrain from eating high-sodium foods.
  • Avoid eating meat especially red meats due to high sodium intake.
  • Drink lots of water and take adequate rest.
  • Alcohol & cigaretttes.
